Our Roots

Crafted for Life, Not the Landfill

Founded in 2021 in Portland, Oregon, Loom & Thread began with a simple question: Can clothing be both beautiful and completely restorative to the earth?

We reject the fast-fashion cycle of synthetic blends and seasonal waste. Instead, we design small, versatile capsules using biodegradable natural fibers that grow softer with every wash.

Every piece is sourced directly from ethical local weavers and regional sewing cooperatives. When you wear Loom & Thread, you support local livelihoods and sustainable agricultural standards.

Elena Rose Founder, Loom & Thread
